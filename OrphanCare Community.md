

#    OrphanCare Community

OrphanCare Community is a simple web-based donation platform
designed to support orphaned children through community
contributions. The platform allows users to donate via **MTN Mobile
Money (MoMo)** or arrange physical delivery of essential items.

## ---

## ##        Project Purpose

This project was built to:

- Connect donors with orphan support needs
- Provide an easy way to donate via mobile payment
- Allow physical donation delivery coordination
- Promote transparency and trust within the community

## ---

## ##     Features


## ###         Home Section
- Inspiring message encouraging community support
- Call-to-action button to view current needs

## ###          Current Needs
Displays urgent support categories:
## - School Supplies
## - Food Support
## - Medical Assistance

Each category includes a **“Help Now”** button that scrolls to the
donation form.

## ---

## ##  Donation Options

Users can choose between:

Pay via Phone (MTN MoMo)
## - Full Name

## - Email
## - Support Type
## - Message
- MTN MoMo Number
- Donation Amount (RWF)

## ###    Deliver Donation
## - Full Name
## - Email
## - Support Type
## - Message
## - Delivery Address
## - Contact Phone
